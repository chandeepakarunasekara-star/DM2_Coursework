# CourseConnect Presentation Outline

## Slide 1 - Title

CourseConnect: Online Course Platform with Oracle and MongoDB

## Slide 2 - Problem and Scope

Students need one place to discover courses, enroll, pay, track progress, access resources, leave feedback, and discuss lessons.

## Slide 3 - System Features

- Course catalogue management
- Lecturer and student management
- Enrollment and registration
- Payment processing
- Progress and certificates
- Course resources, reviews, and discussion forums

## Slide 4 - Database Architecture

Oracle stores transactional data. MongoDB stores flexible content and community interactions.

Show the ER diagram from `docs/ER_DIAGRAM.md`.

## Slide 6 - Oracle Tables and Constraints

Explain primary keys, foreign keys, unique constraints, check constraints, and indexes.

## Slide 7 - PL/SQL Implementation

Explain procedures, functions, triggers, cursors, and exception handling.

## Slide 8 - Five Business Reports

- Popular courses
- Revenue by period
- Student progress
- Pending payments
- Lecturer performance

## Slide 9 - MongoDB Collections

- `courseResources`
- `courseReviews`
- `discussionThreads`

## Slide 10 - MongoDB Queries

Show feedback retrieval, top-rated course aggregation, and forum keyword search.

## Slide 11 - Web Application Demo

Show dashboard, course cards, report summary, and forum search.

## Slide 12 - Conclusion

CourseConnect demonstrates how relational and non-relational databases can work together in a practical learning platform.
